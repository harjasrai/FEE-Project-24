Best Times for Trekking

Discover the ideal times of the year to explore some of the world's most breathtaking trekking destinations.

Mount Everest Base Camp

Best Time: March to May & September to November

Experience moderate temperatures, clear skies, and fewer chances of snowfall.

Inca Trail to Machu Picchu

Best Time: May to September

This dry season offers the most stable weather, ideal for trekking and exploring ancient Incan ruins.

Mount Kilimanjaro

Best Time: January to March & June to October

With clear skies and mild temperatures, these are the best times to summit Africa's tallest peak.
